Installation:

To install the CA-DJ extension, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons store.
  2. Search for "CA-DJ" in the search bar.
  3. Click on the extension and then click on the "Add to [Browser]" button.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Usage:

Once installed, the CA-DJ extension will be accessible through the browser's toolbar. Click on the extension icon to open its settings and enjoy the additional features it provides.
